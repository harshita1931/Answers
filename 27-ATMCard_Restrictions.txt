ATMCard_Restrictions
A customer of some other bank using an SBI ATM can withdraw a maximum of <b><i>Rs.10,000/- per withdrawal.</i></b> A customer of State Bank of India using ATMs of other banks will be able to do <b><i>5 free transactions in a month</i></b> and will have to bear service charges from the 6th transaction of the calendar month onwards.<br><i> For other account holders, all such withdrawals will be charged.</i>
